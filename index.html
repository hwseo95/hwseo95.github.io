Research Interests

Time Series Self-Supervised Learning

Developing novel time series self-supervised learning methods via masked modeling and contrastive learning. Bridging and extending self-supervised learning paradigms from other modalities to time series.

Time Series Foundation Model

Developing domain-specific and general time series foundation models. Enhancing time series tasks by adapting large language models to time series data.

Real-World Applications

Designing domain-specific pretext tasks for solving real-world problems in areas such as transportation, healthcare, and industrial services.
